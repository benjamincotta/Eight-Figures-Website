Digital branding for musicians

For musicans

Focus on making music and perfecting your craft. We'll focus on building your brand to engage and strengthen your fanbase.

We help new and established music artists grow their following by curating unique branding. We handle press kits, merchandising, artwork, photography, social media strategies and much more.

Our aim is to help artists breach that eight figure benchmark, this begins with a solid strategy based on industry and audience insights to get your music heard above the noise and cirulating the streets.

Strategy

Music is a business, a creative busiess. To stand out in the world of streams and views, you need a solid brand focused on encapsulating who you are as an artist. In a way that engages your audience.

Branding

Our graphic designers design logos that instantly resonate with your music and artwork for your projects. We also design all digital materials used to promote your sound.

Press Kits

Every musician needs an EPK (Electronic Press Kit), this gives industry insiders a breakdown of who you are and your most noteable achievements. This includes your artist biography, photos and artwork, logos and branding assets, links to your music as well as press and your contact information.

We build websites for music artists that carries the same energy and vibe you bring from the studio to the stage through engaging design and storeytelling.

Digital

Artist Website, the home of your brand and most valuable digital real estate. Where fans and commerical interests can find the most up-to-date info on your music and tours as well as buy your merchandise.

Social

Social media management and strategy, copywriting, email marketing, press releases and album/single campanigns.

Visuals

Music is now more visual than ever and every shot counts towards promoting your music to the masses. We have a small unit of professional photographers and videographers to capture your visual assets.

We work with artists whose music and energy we resonate with. We help build their vision into an Eight Figure Brand and beyond.

Merchandising

Aside from streams and perfomances, another lucrative income stream is merchandising. Every fan that wears your hoody or snapback promotes your brand and your music. We can help with design and distribution of your merch.

Resources

We have a vast network of music industry professionals from record label execs, promoters, recording studios to venues and artists managers.
